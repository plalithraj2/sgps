About us

Established in the year 1987, SRI GAYATRI POWER SYSTEMS is an ISO 9001:2008 certified provider of a wide range of Electrical products. We are a reputed Manufacturer and Supplier of LED Lighting Systems, Solar Led Lights, Solar Inverters, UPS Systems, Inverters, ARD For Elevators, Battery Chargers, AC-DC Batteries, LED Drivers, Solar Led Street Lights, etc.

Moreover, we are also a key Service Provider of reliable services of Repairing and AMC, UPS, Inverters and Battery Chargers of Any Capacity.Designed from latest technology parts and spares, our products are known in the market for functional efficiency, robustness, and power saving features.

We are a leading company meeting the bulk and urgent demands of the clients with zero defect range with the help of modern manufacturing machines.
